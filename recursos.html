Some useful websites

  • The Hiking Life

    • This site offers extensive information on trip planning, lightweight backpacking techniques, and essential wilderness skills. It's an excellent resource for detailed information on global hiking trails.

  • 12go

    • 12Go Asia is your go-to site for booking all kinds of transport across Southeast Asia. Whether you need a train, bus, ferry, flight, or even a private transfer, they’ve got you covered. The platform makes it super easy to compare prices and find the best deals. Plus, their website and app are really easy to use, and they offer support in multiple languages. You can book in advance to avoid the hassle during peak travel times and read reviews to pick the best options. Their customer service is available 24/7, so help is always at hand. Perfect for stress-free travel planning!

  • Nomadic Fire

    • Nomadic Fire provides a thorough guide for moving abroad, including steps to unlock your phone for use with local SIM cards, setting up a virtual mailbox, and obtaining local currency. The site also offers detailed visa guides and relocation checklists to ensure you don't miss any critical tasks during your move. It emphasizes the importance of advance preparation and organization to ease the transition​

  • Facebook groups

    • Facebook groups are incredibly valuable when moving to a new country because you can connect with thousands of people in the same situation. These groups are full of members who have had the same questions you have now, so you can find information on just about anything that applies to your situation, whether it's looking for a job, getting a residence permit, or opening a bank account quickly and without hassle. Plus, they’re a great way to meet new people and start building your circle of trust in a new place.

  • Meet UP

    • Meetup events are an awesome way to meet new people, make friends, and start building a trusted social circle in your new home. It’s a super chill platform to get in touch with both local people and foreigners. Go check the events you are interested in and sing up to some of them. Yoy won't regret it
