Some pictures from my trips

Wānaka Willow. Located in the town of Wanaka, New Zealand. The tree grows alone in the water

small glacial lake in South Westland, New Zealand, near the township of Fox Glacier. In the background, you can see the majestic snow-capped peaks of Mount Aoraki, New Zealand's highest mountain, and Mount Tasman.

Cliffs of Étretat, in France. This spot is famous for its unique and dramatic white cliffs and rock formations, including the iconic natural arch. It's a favorite place for sightseeing and photography due to its breathtaking coastal views.

Fushimi Inari Taisha in Kyoto. Mountainside Shinto shrine dating from 711 A.D. featuring a path with hundreds of traditional gates. A must do if you visit Kyoto one day.

Landscape of beautiful Conakry the capital of Guinea in western Africa.

Picture of Harukas building from Tennoji park. The tallest bulding in Osaka and the tallest building in Japan until "Azabudai Hills Mori JP Tower" in Tokyo, seized the title.
